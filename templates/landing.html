AlphaBotix Trading provides subscription-based algorithmic trading software for retail investors. We integrate with third-party brokerage APIs (such as Alpaca and OKX) to execute automated strategies. We do not hold client funds; we provide the automated execution tools.

What we are

AlphaBotix Trading is a software platform operated by Walter Landscaping L.L.C. Customers connect their own brokerage accounts and use our bots to automate trading decisions. Custody of cash and securities remains with the customer’s broker at all times.
